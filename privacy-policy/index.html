TapType Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 22, 2025

Overview

TapType ("we", "our", or "the app") is a typing game application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

TapType does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Information We Collect

TapType does not collect any personal information. All game data, including:

  • Game scores and statistics
  • Settings preferences (theme, sound, haptics)
  • Purchase status

is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to external servers.

Third-Party Services

Apple App Store: If you make in-app purchases, these transactions are processed by Apple through the App Store. Apple's privacy policy governs how they handle payment information. We do not have access to your payment details.

TapType does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using iOS's standard storage mechanisms (UserDefaults). This data:

  • Remains on your device
  • Is not synced to any cloud service by the app
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Can be reset within the app's settings

Children's Privacy

TapType does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
